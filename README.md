# multiplayer-game-pricing-analysis
Analysis of pricing trends in multiplayer games, comparing paid (P2P) and free-to-play (F2P) titles over time.


This project analyzes the release prices of multiplayer games from 2021 to 2025, focusing on the differences between paid and free-to-play games. It examines yearly stability, monthly volatility, seasonal trends, and market composition. The analysis includes statistical measures such as mean and standard deviation to understand pricing behavior and guide consumers on optimal purchase timing. Visualizations and insights are provided to highlight trends in the multiplayer gaming market.
